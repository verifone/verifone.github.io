Detailed Description

Requests various types of reports and other status information.

Allows closure of the current reporting period, querying of the reporting capabilities of the terminal, and settlement/reconciliation with the host/acquirer(s). Please note that the events for these methods are sent to general listeners, as these commands are sent outside of the context of a session.
