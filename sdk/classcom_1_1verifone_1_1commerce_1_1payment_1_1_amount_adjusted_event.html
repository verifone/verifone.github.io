Detailed Description

When the amount is adjusted by applications in response to the com.verifone.commerce.triggers.AmountAdjustmentRequest or com.verifone.commerce.triggers.PaymentAmountAdjustmentRequest Triggers, the adjustments proposed are returned in this event. This allows the POS application to keep track of changes and provides the opportunity to decide which adjustments are applied in the response.
