◆ getEncryptedTracks()

abstract String getEncryptedTracks ( )
abstract

Construction of field EncryptedTracks: Plaintext track data are concatenated as follows:

<FS><Track-1><FS><Track-2><FS><Track-3><FS><ExpirationDate><FS>

Where

<FS>

is ASCII 0x1C (file separator character) and is mandatory. Tracks 1, 2, 3 and ExpirationDate are optional. ExpirationDate is in the format MMYY, where MM is two digit month and YY is two digit year. For example the expiration date is Sep 2018, it is represented as 0918. The concatenated data is encrypted and converted to a HEX String.
